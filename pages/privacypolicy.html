Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1st, 2025

At Thriftly, we value and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, make a purchase, or interact with us in any other way. By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that helps us improve your shopping experience, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, Email address, Shipping and billing address, Phone number, Payment details (processed securely through third-party payment processors).
  • Usage Data: We also collect information about how you interact with our website, such as IP address, Browser type, Device type, Pages visited, Referring/exit pages, Date and time of visits, Click-through and engagement data.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences, track your shopping cart, and improve site performance. You can control cookie settings through your browser, but some site features may not function properly if you disable cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for several purposes, including:

  • Processing Orders: To complete your purchases and deliver your items.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, provide assistance, and resolve issues.
  • Marketing: To send you promotional emails and newsletters (with your consent).
  • Improving Services: To analyze how users interact with our website and improve our products and services.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted third-party service providers for the following purposes:

  • Payment Processing: We use secure, third-party payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal) to handle transactions. These processors may collect and store your payment information according to their own privacy policies.
  • Shipping Providers: We may share your address and contact information with carriers like USPS, UPS, or FedEx to deliver your order.
  • Analytics Services: We may use third-party analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our website.

We may also disclose your information if required by law, to protect our rights, or in connection with business transactions (e.g., mergers or acquisitions).

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Correction: You can request access to the information we hold about you and update any inaccuracies.
  • Opt-Out: You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by clicking the “unsubscribe” link at the bottom of our emails.
  • Data Deletion: If you no longer want us to retain your personal data, you can request deletion, subject to legal or operational restrictions.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at [contact@thriftly.com].

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
