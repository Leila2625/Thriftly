Accessibility Statement

Thriftly is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including those with disabilities. We strive to adhere to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 to improve usability and accessibility across our platform.

Our Accessibility Efforts

To provide an inclusive experience, we are implementing the following accessibility features:

  • Keyboard navigation and screen reader compatibility
  • Sufficient color contrast and text readability
  • Alternative text for images
  • Regularly reviewing and updating our accessibility features

Feedback and Support

We are continuously working to enhance accessibility and welcome your feedback. If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us at contact@thriftly.com.

Your feedback is invaluable to us as we continue to improve and create a more accessible experience for all.
