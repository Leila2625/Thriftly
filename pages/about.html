Our Story

At Thriftly, we believe that fashion should not only be stylish but also sustainable. Our journey began with a simple idea: to reduce fashion waste and provide affordable, high-quality clothing to conscious consumers. What started as a personal passion for thrifting and upcycling has grown into a thriving online community where vintage finds, pre-loved clothes, and creatively upcycled pieces take center stage. We’re dedicated to offering unique, one-of-a-kind items that are both trendy and timeless, all while contributing to a more sustainable world. Our commitment to sustainable fashion is at the heart of everything we do, helping you dress well without compromising the planet.

Sustainability Goals

Helping the Environment: By reducing the demand for new clothing production, we’re actively contributing to the reduction of textile waste, water usage, and harmful emissions. Through thrifting and upcycling, we minimize the environmental footprint of fashion.

Increasing Availability of Affordable Clothing: We believe sustainable fashion should be accessible to all. By offering stylish, affordable second-hand and upcycled clothing, we ensure that everyone can enjoy eco-conscious fashion without breaking the bank.

Supporting Small Businesses and Creators: We are dedicated to promoting local artisans and small businesses by offering unique, upcycled pieces crafted by independent creators. This helps foster creativity and supports eco-friendly practices within our community.
